Brooklyn Tech x Stuy Speaker Series!

The Freshman Caucus is excited to kick off our Speaker Series, a series of professionals that will be invited to share interesting information about their craft. Our first speaker is Dr. David Deitcher, a Cornell neuroscience professor who will come in on Thursday, March 18th at 4:30 PM. He will be doing a presentation about seizures and epilepsy, including the “julius seizure” gene he helped discover. There will also be a chance for you to ask questions to Dr. Deitcher at the end.

Ms. Maggio and Mrs. Garber will be offering extra credit for the students that attend! If you want to receive extra credit for her class, you must come to the event, take notes on what you heard, and then submit the notes through the exit survey we will provide. We hope to see you there! - FC External Outreach Team
