    Hiro, our Director of Operations, is key to keeping the caucus running. He makes sure that every department is doing what is expected of them, and helps to answer questions that any members or department heads might have. He also serves as a source of communication between departments, guiding the work on joint projects and occasionally collaborating with specific departments on some of the more important assignments. He functions as an extension of the President and Vice President; a person that members can go to with questions or for clarification on their responsibilities.

Gordon Ebanks

Director of Communication

Email: gordon.ebanks@stuysu.org

  • Facebook


  • Learn More

    The Department of Communication, led by Gordon Ebanks, bridges the gap between the caucus and the student body. They reflect our ideas, plans, successes and upcoming events to all of the freshman, usually through emails, posters/flyers, or facebook posts. By keeping in constant contact with the wants and needs of the students, these members make sure that we know how our peers feel about our decisions, and what we can do to represent everyone to the best of our abilities.

Benjamin Shpetner

Director of Finance

Email: benjamin.shpetner@stuysu.org

  • Facebook


  • Learn More

    The Department of Finance handles the money that the caucus makes and spends. They work closely with other departments, allotting funds when necessary, especially with the Department of Event Planning. They make sure that the caucus profits from events like dances and there is student satisfaction by determining ticket prices, etc. Led by Benjamin Shpetner, the team works especially on budgeting and cost revenue analysis; telling us what we can and cannot spend money on.
