    The Department of Event Planning organizes all of the events that the Freshman Caucus hosts. They decide what will get the Freshman Class interacting with the Caucus the most, and they plan and organize accordingly. They will help execute and brainstorm the events alongside the presidents of freshman caucus. Uniquely, this department is run by two directors - they have the responsibility to collaborate and agree on plans with their partner as well as their normal duties.

Joyti Nath

Director of Internal Outreach

Email: joyti.nath@stuysu.org

  • Facebook


  • Learn More

    The Department of Internal Outreach is vital to connecting with the freshman student body. This department is divided in three subdivisions: The Social Media Managers, the Graphic Designer, and the Newsletter Team. They reflect ideas and plans through social media posts, digital posters, and emails, respectively. These three sub-departments must work together to ensure that Internal Affairs are working smoothly. Joyti will oversee all operations in the three sub-departments, and make sure every member is actively contributing and working.
